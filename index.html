01 — About
From code
to product.
By choice.

I started as a backend engineer — writing code, living the constraints, understanding the tradeoffs. That foundation permanently changed how I think about product.

Over 7+ years I've moved from building retail systems at Cognizant, to Business Analyst and then Product Manager at Deloitte — owning full product surfaces at the intersection of AI, enterprise SaaS, and consumer scale.

Today I'm a PM at Almosafer — one of the GCC's largest OTAs — driving MarTech and growth across Homepage, SEO, and Communications. Before that, I shipped a knowledge-graph GenAI integration at Deloitte that hit 95% accuracy and won an award for product excellence.

I work best at the edge of technical complexity and real business impact. Driven by data, comfortable with ambiguity.
